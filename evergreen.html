Evergreen

What's it like living in Evergreen?

Evergreen is a neighborhood in San Jose, California with a population of 67,820. Evergreen is in Santa Clara County and is one of the best places to live in California. Living in Evergreen offers residents a sparse suburban feel and most residents own their homes. In Evergreen there are a lot of coffee shops and parks. Many families and young professionals live in Evergreen and residents tend to be liberal. The public schools in Evergreen are highly rated.

Evergreen is located in East San Jose nestled in and around the valleys of the East Foothills. The area has experienced rapid development, which has led to multiple communities and private country clubs. Visitors can experience shopping at Eastridge Mall, golfing at The Ranch Golf Club, and events at Reid-Hillview Airport. The Evergreen area of San Jose includes many newer homes and communities. It is a desirable Silicon Valley community as Evergreen is scenic and most of the district enjoys highly regarded schools, particularly at the elementary and middle school levels.
